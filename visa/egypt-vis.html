Egypt Visa for Indian Travelers: Complete Guide

Egypt, land of ancient civilizations, pyramids, and the majestic Nile, is one of the most fascinating destinations in the world. Indian travelers require a visa to enter Egypt.

Skyway Tours and Travels ensures a hassle-free visa application so you can focus on exploring Egypt’s rich history and cultural treasures.

Do Indian Citizens Need a Visa for Egypt?

Yes, Indian passport holders require a visa to enter Egypt. E-visa and visa on arrival options are available for Indian travelers.

Types of Egypt Visas for Indian Travelers

1. Tourist Visa

  • Best for: Sightseeing, cultural exploration, and historical tours
  • Validity: Typically up to 30 days
  • Requirement: Online e-visa application or visa on arrival

2. Business Visa

  • Best for: Business meetings, conferences, and professional purposes
  • Validity: Usually up to 90 days
  • Requirement: Invitation letter from an Egyptian company or organization

Main Attractions and Things to Do in Egypt

1. Cairo & Giza

  • Explore the Pyramids of Giza, Sphinx, Egyptian Museum, and bustling markets.

2. Luxor & Karnak

  • Visit Luxor Temple, Karnak Temple, and the Valley of the Kings for ancient history.

3. Aswan & Nile Cruises

  • Enjoy serene Nile river cruises and visit Philae Temple and Nubian villages.

4. Alexandria

  • Discover the Mediterranean coast, Alexandria Library, and Qaitbay Citadel.

5. Red Sea Resorts

  • Relax in Hurghada or Sharm El Sheikh with snorkeling, diving, and water sports.

Important Notes

  • Passport must be valid for at least 6 months from entry.
  • Carry a printed copy of your visa or e-visa confirmation.
  • Ensure visa type matches your travel purpose (tourist or business).

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Can Indian citizens apply for an Egypt e-visa online?

Yes, Indian travelers can apply online for an Egypt e-visa.

2. How long is an Egypt tourist visa valid?

Typically up to 30 days for single entry.

3. What documents are required?

Passport valid for at least 6 months, completed visa form, photographs, travel itinerary, or invitation letter.

4. How long does visa processing take?

E-visa processing usually takes 3–5 working days; visa on arrival is processed at the airport.
