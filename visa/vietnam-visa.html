Vietnam Visa for Indian Travelers: Complete Guide

Vietnam, known for its stunning landscapes, vibrant cities, and rich history, is a must-visit destination in Southeast Asia. Indian travelers require a visa to enter Vietnam.

Skyway Tours and Travels ensures a seamless visa application process so you can enjoy Vietnam’s beauty without any hassle.

Do Indian Citizens Need a Visa for Vietnam?

Yes, Indian passport holders require a visa to enter Vietnam. You can apply online for an e-visa or opt for a visa on arrival if entering by air.

Types of Vietnam Visas for Indian Travelers

1. E-Visa (Online Visa)

  • Best for: Tourists and short business trips
  • Validity: 30 days (single entry)
  • Processing Time: 3–5 working days
  • Entry Points: International airports and select land borders

2. Visa on Arrival (VOA)

  • Best for: Travelers entering Vietnam by air
  • Validity: 30–90 days
  • Requirement: Approval letter from authorized agency

Main Attractions and Things to Do in Vietnam

1. Ha Long Bay

  • Take a cruise among limestone islands and enjoy kayaking or swimming.

2. Hanoi

  • Explore Old Quarter, Hoan Kiem Lake, and historical temples.

3. Ho Chi Minh City

  • Visit War Remnants Museum, Cu Chi Tunnels, and vibrant markets.

4. Hoi An

  • Stroll through lantern-lit streets, ancient houses, and riverside cafes.

5. Sapa

  • Trek in terraced rice fields and visit ethnic minority villages.

Important Notes

  • Passport must be valid for at least 6 months from entry.
  • Carry a printed copy of your visa or approval letter.
  • Ensure visa type matches your travel purpose (tourist or business).

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Can Indian citizens apply for a Vietnam e-visa online?

Yes, Indian travelers can apply online for a Vietnam e-visa.

2. How long is a Vietnam tourist visa valid?

Typically 30 days for e-visa or up to 90 days for visa on arrival.

3. What documents are required?

Passport valid for at least 6 months, completed visa application, photographs, travel itinerary, and approval letter for VOA.

4. How long does visa processing take?

E-visa: 3–5 working days, VOA approval letter: 2–3 working days.
