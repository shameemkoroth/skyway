Malaysia Visa for Indian Travelers: Complete Guide

Malaysia, a vibrant Southeast Asian country, is known for modern cities, lush rainforests, and pristine islands. Indian travelers require a visa to visit Malaysia.

Skyway Tours and Travels ensures a smooth visa application process so you can focus on exploring Malaysia’s culture, cuisine, and natural beauty.

Do Indian Citizens Need a Visa for Malaysia?

Yes, Indian passport holders require a visa to enter Malaysia. You can apply for an e-visa online or obtain a visa on arrival under certain conditions.

Types of Malaysia Visas for Indian Travelers

1. Tourist Visa

  • Best for: Sightseeing, leisure, and vacation trips
  • Validity: Usually up to 30 days
  • Requirement: Online e-visa application or visa on arrival

2. Business Visa

  • Best for: Business meetings, conferences, and professional purposes
  • Validity: Typically up to 90 days
  • Requirement: Invitation letter from a Malaysian company or organization

Main Attractions and Things to Do in Malaysia

1. Kuala Lumpur

  • Visit the iconic Petronas Twin Towers, Batu Caves, and Bukit Bintang shopping district.

2. Penang

  • Explore George Town’s heritage streets, street food, and historical landmarks.

3. Langkawi

  • Relax on beautiful beaches, enjoy water sports, and ride the Langkawi Sky Bridge.

4. Malacca

  • Discover colonial architecture, riverside cafes, and historical sites.

5. Borneo (Sabah & Sarawak)

  • Experience rainforests, wildlife, and Mount Kinabalu climbing adventures.

Important Notes

  • Passport must be valid for at least 6 months from entry.
  • Carry a printed copy of your visa or e-visa confirmation.
  • Ensure visa type matches your travel purpose (tourist or business).

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Can Indian citizens apply for a Malaysia e-visa online?

Yes, Indian travelers can apply online for a Malaysia e-visa.

2. How long is a Malaysia tourist visa valid?

Typically up to 30 days for single entry.

3. What documents are required?

Passport valid for at least 6 months, completed visa form, photographs, travel itinerary, or invitation letter.

4. How long does visa processing take?

E-visa processing usually takes 3–5 working days; visa on arrival is processed at the airport.
