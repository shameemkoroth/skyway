Armenia Visa for Indian Travelers: Complete Guide

Planning a trip to Yerevan, Lake Sevan, or the historic monasteries of Armenia? Indian travelers need a visa to enter Armenia.

Skyway Tours and Travels offers hassle-free visa assistance to make your Armenia travel experience smooth and convenient.

Do Indian Citizens Need a Visa for Armenia?

Yes, Indian passport holders require a visa to enter Armenia. The most convenient way is to apply online via Armenia’s e-visa system.

Types of Armenia Visas for Indian Travelers

1. Tourist e-Visa

  • Best for: Tourism and short visits
  • Validity: 21 days (single entry)
  • Processing Time: 3–5 working days
  • Entry Points: Zvartnots International Airport and other major checkpoints

Documents Required:

  • Valid Indian passport (minimum 6 months validity)
  • Passport-size photograph
  • Completed visa application form
  • Travel itinerary or hotel bookings
  • Valid email for visa delivery

2. Business Visa

  • Best for: Business meetings or conferences
  • Validity: Typically up to 90 days
  • Requirement: Invitation letter from an Armenian company

Armenia Visa Processing Time

  • e-Visa: 3–5 working days
  • Business Visa: 5–7 working days depending on the application

Skyway Tip: Apply at least 7–10 days before your travel date to ensure timely approval.

Important Notes

  • Passport must be valid for at least 6 months from your date of entry.
  • Carry a printed copy of your e-visa or embassy visa approval.
  • Visa type must match your travel purpose.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Can I get an Armenia e-visa for tourism?

Yes, Indian citizens can apply online for a tourist e-visa to visit Armenia.

2. How long is an Armenia tourist visa valid?

Most tourist e-visas are valid for 21 days from the date of entry.

3. What documents are required for an Armenia e-visa?

Passport valid for at least 6 months, passport-size photo, travel itinerary, and completed visa application form.

4. How long does it take to process an Armenia visa?

e-Visas typically take 3–5 working days; business visas may take 5–7 days.

5. Can I extend my Armenia visa?

Extensions are possible through Armenia’s immigration office, subject to approval.
