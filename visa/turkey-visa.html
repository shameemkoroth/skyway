Turkey Visa for Indian Travelers: Complete Guide

Dreaming of Istanbul’s historic sites, Cappadocia’s fairy chimneys, or Antalya’s Mediterranean beaches? Indian travelers require a visa to enter Turkey.

Skyway Tours and Travels ensures your Turkey visa application is smooth, fast, and hassle-free.

Do Indian Citizens Need a Visa for Turkey?

Yes, Indian passport holders require a visa to enter Turkey. The e-visa is the most convenient option for short tourist or business trips.

Types of Turkey Visas for Indian Travelers

1. e-Visa (Electronic Visa)

  • Best for: Tourism and short business trips
  • Validity: 30 days (single entry)
  • Processing Time: 24–48 hours
  • Entry Points: All international airports in Turkey

Documents Required:

  • Valid Indian passport (minimum 6 months validity)
  • Passport-size photograph
  • Travel itinerary
  • Valid email for e-visa delivery
  • Credit/debit card for online payment

2. Sticker Visa (via Embassy)

  • Best for: Longer stays, work, or study purposes
  • Validity: Depending on the purpose, up to 90 days or multiple entries
  • Requirement: Embassy application and supporting documents

Turkey Visa Processing Time

  • e-Visa: Usually approved within 24–48 hours
  • Embassy Visa: 5–10 working days depending on application type

Skyway Tip: Apply at least 5–7 days before your travel date to avoid delays.

Important Notes

  • Passport must be valid for at least 6 months from your date of entry.
  • Carry a printed copy of your e-visa or embassy visa approval.
  • Visa type must match your travel purpose (tourist, business, or study).

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Can I get a Turkey e-visa for tourism?

Yes, Indian citizens can apply online for a Turkey e-visa for tourism or short business trips.

2. How long is a Turkey e-visa valid?

Most e-visas are valid for 30 days, single entry, starting from the date of entry.

3. What documents are required for a Turkey e-visa?

Passport valid for at least 6 months, travel itinerary, passport-size photo, and a credit/debit card for payment.

4. How long does it take to process a Turkey e-visa?

Typically within 24–48 hours. Embassy visas may take 5–10 working days.

5. Can I extend my Turkey visa?

Extensions are generally not available for e-visas; longer stays require a sticker visa via the embassy.
