Tajikistan Visa for Indian Travelers: Complete Guide

Planning a trip to explore the Pamir Mountains, Iskanderkul Lake, or the historic city of Dushanbe? Indian travelers require a visa to enter Tajikistan.

Skyway Tours and Travels provides expert assistance to make your Tajikistan visa application smooth and hassle-free.

Do Indian Citizens Need a Visa for Tajikistan?

Yes, Indian passport holders require a visa to enter Tajikistan. You can apply online via e-visa or at the Tajikistan Embassy.

Types of Tajikistan Visas for Indian Travelers

1. Tourist Visa

  • Best for: Tourism and sightseeing
  • Validity: 30 days (single entry)
  • Processing Time: 3–5 working days
  • Entry Points: International airports and land borders

Documents Required:

  • Valid Indian passport (minimum 6 months validity)
  • Passport-size photograph
  • Completed visa application form
  • Travel itinerary and hotel bookings
  • Proof of sufficient funds

2. Business Visa

  • Best for: Business meetings or conferences
  • Validity: 30–90 days (single/multiple entry)
  • Requirement: Invitation letter from a Tajik company

Kyrgyzstan Visa Processing Time

  • Business Visa: 3–7 working days

Skyway Tip: Apply at least 7–10 days before your travel date.

Important Notes

  • Passport must be valid for at least 6 months from your date of entry.
  • Carry a printed copy of your visa or e-visa confirmation.
  • Visa type must match your travel purpose.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Can I get a Tajikistan visa on arrival?

Yes, in certain cases visa on arrival is available, but applying online via e-visa is recommended for faster entry.

2. How long is a Tajikistan tourist visa valid?

Tourist visas are generally valid for 30 days from the date of entry.

3. What documents are required for a Tajikistan e-visa?

Passport valid for at least 6 months, passport-size photo, completed visa application, travel itinerary, and proof of funds.

4. How long does it take to process a Tajikistan visa?

Typically 3–5 working days for a tourist visa and 3–7 days for a business visa.

5. Can I extend my Tajikistan visa?

Yes, extensions are possible at the Tajikistan immigration office, subject to approval.
