Schengen Visa Essentials for Indian Citizens

Dreaming of a European adventure? With a Schengen Visa, Indian travelers can explore 29 countries across Europe using a single permit.

This guide walks you through visa categories, eligibility, documentation, the 90/180-day rule, and the complete application process.

Understanding the Schengen Visa

The Schengen Visa allows short-term travel across participating European nations for up to 90 days within any 180-day period.

Visa Categories for Indian Nationals

  • Type C (Short Stay): For tourism, business, or family visits up to 90 days.
  • Type A (Airport Transit): For transiting through Schengen airports without entering the country.
  • Type B (Transit): For brief land or sea transit through Schengen territory.
  • Type D (National Visa): For long-term stays over 90 days for study, work, or family reunification.
  • LTV (Limited Territorial Validity): Valid only in specific Schengen countries under special circumstances.

Required Documents

  • Valid passport (minimum 6 months validity, 3 blank pages)
  • Completed and signed visa application form
  • Two recent passport-sized photographs
  • Cover letter explaining travel purpose and duration
  • Bank statements (last 6 months)
  • Income Tax Returns/Form 16 (last 3 years)
  • Confirmed round-trip flight tickets
  • Hotel booking confirmation
  • Detailed travel itinerary
  • Travel insurance (minimum coverage of EUR 30,000)

Employment Documentation:

  • For Employees: Leave approval and last 3 salary slips
  • For Self-Employed: Business registration, MOA, GST certificate

Application Process

  1. Prepare Documents: Gather all required paperwork and proofs.
  2. Book Appointment: Schedule a slot via the embassy or consulate website.
  3. Attend Interview: Submit documents and biometric data.
  4. Wait for Processing: Visa decisions typically take 15–25 working days.

Processing Timeline

  • Duration: 15–25 days
  • Maximum Stay: 90 days
  • Visa Validity: Up to 6 months
  • Entry Type: Single or multiple entries

Advantages of a Tourist Visa

  • Explore Multiple Nations: One visa covers several countries
  • Convenience: Simplified travel planning
  • Faster Border Clearance: Reduced wait times at immigration

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Schengen Visa?

It permits Indian travelers to visit 29 European countries with a single visa.

What documents are needed?

Passport, insurance, bookings, financial proof, and itinerary are essential.

How long is the processing time?

Typically 15–25 working days. Apply early.

How long can I stay?

Up to 90 days within any 180-day period.

Can I reapply after rejection?

Yes, after addressing the reasons for denial.

Is travel insurance mandatory?

Yes, with a minimum coverage of EUR 30,000.
