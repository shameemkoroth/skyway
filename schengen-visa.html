Schengen Visa: Requirements, Fees and Application Process

Planning to explore Europe’s diverse cultures, historic landmarks, and scenic landscapes? Indian citizens applying for Schengen Visa are in a position to move freely across 29 member states of the Schengen Area using only one permit. Schengen Visa makes travelling more convenient by eliminating the need to obtain a variety of national visas and instead obtaining a single document, which can be used to travel in any country within the Schengen area whether the trip is tourism, business related or a transit.

This comprehensive guide is designed to assist Indian applicants with detailed information on visa types, eligibility criteria, required documents, the 90/180-day stay rule and the step-by-step application process. It includes information on how to prepare sufficient financial evidence, purchase travel insurance, and meet the requirements of the embassy, among other things, thus providing applicants with the entire range of tools to enhance their chances of successful results. If you’re an Indian traveler looking to experience Europe with ease and confidence, understanding the Visa process is your first step.
