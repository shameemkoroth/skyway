Your Ultimate Guide to a Cambodia Visa

Dreaming of exploring the majestic temples of Angkor Wat or the bustling streets of Phnom Penh? If you're an Indian citizen, you'll need a valid Cambodia Visa to make that journey a reality.

The good news? Getting your Cambodian visa is straightforward and can often be done conveniently online! Whether you're planning a thrilling tourist getaway, a quick business trip, or simply passing through, this guide covers everything you need.

Cambodia Visa Requirements for Indian Citizens

Prepare the following documents to ensure smooth processing:

DocumentDetails
Valid PassportMust be valid for 6 months beyond entry date into Cambodia.
Photograph2 recent color photos (4×6 cm) with white background.
Application FormAccurate details matching your passport.
Travel ItineraryConfirmed round-trip tickets.
Accommodation ProofHotel reservation or host invitation letter.
