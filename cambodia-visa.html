Cambodia Visa Requirements: Your Checklist

A complete application is the first step to approval. Gather the following mandatory documents:

Document Category Details Required for Indian Applicants
1. Valid Passport Must be valid for at least 6 months beyond your entry date into Cambodia. Ensure you have at least one to two blank pages for visa stamping.
2. Recent Photographs 2 recent color passport-size photos (typically 4 Ã— 6 cm) with a clear white background.
3. Application Form A duly filled and signed Cambodia visa application form. All information must be accurate and match your passport.
4. Travel Itinerary Confirmed round-trip flight tickets showing both your entry into and exit from Cambodia.
5. Accommodation Proof A confirmed hotel reservation or an invitation letter proving where you will stay during your trip.
6. Financial Documents Latest bank statements (from the last 3-6 months) to demonstrate you have sufficient funds to cover your travel expenses.
7. Cover Letter A personal letter stating your purpose of travel, travel dates, and your clear intention to return to India after your visit.

Special Requirements

    • Business Visa: Official invitation letter from a Cambodian host company and proof of company registration/trade license.
    • Student Visa:Admission letter from the educational institution and financial proof for tuition and living expenses.

Note: The Royal Cambodian Embassy reserves the right to request further documentation based on the application profile.

Indian citizens benefit from multiple convenient visa options:

Cambodia Tourist Visa (T) Valid for a 30-day stay. Available via online e-Visa or Visa on Arrival (VOA).
Cambodia e-Visa The quickest option for tourism. Apply online; processing time is typically 2-3 business days.
Visa on Arrival (VOA) Issued directly at designated international airports and major land borders.
Cambodia Business Visa (E) For work or professional visits. Initial validity is often 30 days, but it is extendable up to 1 year within Cambodia.

How to Apply for Your Cambodia Visa from India

The easiest route for Indian tourists is the e-Visa or Visa on Arrival.

E-Visa (Recommended Tourist Option)

    • Processing Time: Fast, typically 2-3 business days.
    • Stay Period: Up to 30 days.
    • Validity: The e-Visa is valid for3 months from the date of issue (single entry).

Visa on Arrival (VOA)

    • Available at major international airports (Phnom Penh, Siem Reap) and select land border crossings.
    • Requires a valid passport, a recent photograph, and payment of the visa fee

Avoid Rejection: Common Visa Denial Reasons

Ensure your application is flawless by avoiding these common mistakes:

      1. Incomplete/Incorrect Documentation: Any missing forms, blurred photos, or inconsistent data will lead to rejection.

      2. Insufficient Financial Proof: Failure to convincingly demonstrate funds to cover your trip and return travel.

      3. Passport Validity Issues: Your passport must be valid for at least six months from your arrival date.

      4. Unclear Purpose of Visit: Lack of supporting documents to confirm the reason for your travel (e.g., missing hotel bookings for a tourist visa).

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. Do Indians need a visa for Cambodia?

Yes. Indian citizens must secure a valid visa before or upon arrival.

2. What are the application options?

You can apply via e-Visa (online), Visa on Arrival (VOA), or at the Cambodian Embassy/Consulate.

3. How long is the Tourist Visa stay?

The standard Tourist Visa (T) and e-Visa allow a single stay of up to 30 days.

4. How fast is the e-Visa processing?

E-Visa approval is typically granted within 2-3 business days. VOA is immediate.

5. What key documents are needed?

Valid passport (6+ months), application form, recent photo, confirmed flight tickets, and accommodation proof.
