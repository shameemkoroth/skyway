Why Get a Schengen Visa?

Beyond just granting entry, the Schengen Visa offers several benefits...

  • Multi-Country Travel: Visit multiple countries with one visa, eliminating the hassle of applying to each nation separately.
  • Time and Effort Savings: Significantly reduces the bureaucratic effort needed for an extensive European trip.
  • Streamlined Border Entry: Enjoy faster and easier transit across internal Schengen borders, meaning less time waiting and more time exploring!

FAQs

What is a Schengen Visa?

A Schengen Visa allows Indian travelers to visit 29 countries in Europe with one visa.

How long does it take to process?

Typically 15–25 working days.
