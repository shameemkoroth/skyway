Types of Azerbaijan Visas for Indian Citizens

Azerbaijan attracts visitors for a variety of reasons. Choosing the correct visa type based on your purpose of travel is crucial. Here are the most common options:

  1. Azerbaijan Tourist Visa
    • Ideal for: Leisure, sightseeing, and cultural exploration.
    • Details: Typically a single-entry visa allowing a stay of up to 30 days. The application is usually done quickly and easily online via the official e-Visa portal.
  2. Azerbaijan Business Visa
    • Ideal for: Work, official meetings, conferences, or trade.
    • Details: Can be single-entry or multiple-entry, with validity ranging from 30 days up to a year. Requires an official invitation from an Azerbaijani company or organization.
  3. Azerbaijan Transit Visa
    • Ideal for: Travelers passing through Azerbaijan to a third destination.
    • Details: Generally valid for up to 5 days. Requires proof of your onward journey (e.g., flight or train tickets).
  4. Azerbaijan Student Visa
    • Ideal for: Indian students enrolling in educational institutions in Azerbaijan.
    • Details: Allows for a stay throughout the duration of your studies and requires documents like an acceptance letter and proof of financial support.
  5. Azerbaijan Work Visa
    • Ideal for: Indian citizens with employment contracts in Azerbaijan.
    • Details: Issued based on an employment contract and requires sponsorship from an Azerbaijani employer. The duration depends on the job terms.

Essential Documents for Your Azerbaijan Visa

To proceed with your application, you will need to gather the following key documents:

  • Original Passport: Must have a minimum validity of 6 months from your date of arrival and at least 2 blank pages. Include all old passports, if applicable.
  • Photographs: Two recent, scanned color photographs.
  • Visa Application Forms: Fully completed and signed.
  • Personal Covering Letter: Stating the purpose of your travel.
  • Financial Proof: Original, stamped, and updated Bank Statement for the last 3 months with the bank seal.
  • Travel Itinerary: Evidence of return flight tickets and hotel reservations for your entire stay.
