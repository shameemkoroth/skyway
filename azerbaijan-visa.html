What is the Schengen Visa?

The Schengen Visa is a **short-stay permit** (Type C) that allows travelers to visit any of the 29 countries in the Schengen Area. The primary rule to remember is the **"90/180-day rule"**: you can spend a maximum of **90 days** in any of these countries within a **180-day** time frame. It is the key to multi-country European travel!

Schengen Visa Types for Indian Citizens

Visa Type Category Purpose & Stay Limit
Short Stay (Type C) Tourist, Business, Visit For stays up to 90 days within a 180-day period. (The most common type for holidays).
Airport Transit (Type A) Transit Required if you are transiting through the international zone of a Schengen airport.
National Visa (Type D) Long Stay For stays over 90 days (e.g., for study, long-term employment, research, or family reunification).
Limited Territorial Validity (LTV) Special Cases Issued only for specific Schengen countries in exceptional circumstances.

Document Checklist: Your Application Toolkit

Ensure a smooth application by having all mandatory documents ready. *Always check the specific country's embassy website for the most current and exact requirements.*

Personal & Travel Documents

  • Original Passport: At least 6 months validity & minimum 2 blank pages.
  • Duly Signed and filled application form.
  • 2 Recent Colour Photographs (passport size).
  • Confirmed Return Flight Tickets & **Hotel Reservations** (Proof of accommodation).
  • **Day-wise Itinerary:** A detailed plan of your trip.

Financial & Intent Documents

  • Cover Letter: Detailed purpose and duration of the trip.
  • Bank Statement: Last 6 months.
  • Income Tax Returns/Form 16: For the last 3 years.

Mandatory Insurance & Employment Proof

  • Travel Insurance: Minimum EUR 30,000 coverage for medical repatriation, urgent care, and emergency hospital treatment, valid across the entire Schengen Area.
  • **Employment Proof (If Employed):** Leave certificate/NOC from employer and last 3 months' salary slips.
  • **Employment Proof (If Self-Employed):** Business license, Memorandum of Association (MOA), and GST certificate.

Application Process: Your 4-Step Guide from India

  1. Gather Required Documents: Collect your passport, financial proofs, travel insurance, and all supporting documents as per the checklist above.
  2. Schedule an Appointment: Visit the website of the relevant embassy/consulate or their authorized visa application centre (like VFS Global) and book your slot.
  3. Attend the Visa Appointment: Submit your application file (originals and copies), pay the applicable fees, and provide your biometric data (fingerprints and photo) if not previously collected.
  4. Await Visa Processing: Track your application status and wait for your passport to be returned.
