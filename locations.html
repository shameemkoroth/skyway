Discover The Beautiful World

Kashmir

Known as "Heaven on Earth," Kashmir stands out as North India's most spectacular travel destination. You simply won't find scenic landscapes, icy glaciers, pristine lakes, and lofty mountains as breathtaking as these anywhere else in the country. The very ambiance of Kashmir redefines serenity and tranquility. With a unique charm in every season—be it summer, winter, or monsoon—Kashmir is always welcoming. Discovering its numerous natural wonders, rich history, unique culture, and delicious cuisines will truly elevate your journey through Incredible India.

Istanbul

Istanbul is where history meets the future. Spanning two continents, this majestic city is a vibrant tapestry of historical and cultural riches, having served as the center for countless civilizations. Beyond its charming natural beauty, Istanbul is the symbol of a dynamic, modern metropolis. Its multicultural energy blends the past and future into one exhilarating pot, offering visitors a profound sense of universal history and adventure at every turn.

Paris

Paris is the height of sophistication, a city that welcomes everyone from the luxury traveler to the budget explorer. Beyond the iconic Eiffel Tower, the city's spacious boulevards are lined with countless gorgeous landmarks, monuments, and charming cafes. The world-famous French cuisine reaches its peak here, with culinary delights available on every corner. From the intricate design of its bridges to its grand public buildings, Paris offers stunning sights and impressive architecture that have made it a world-renowned symbol of beauty and art.

Bali

Bali is a destination where natural beauty meets spiritual depth. To match the island's captivating scenery, the range of accommodations is simply outstanding. Whether you seek a luxurious beachfront resort, a stylish affordable hostel, or a private villa rented at an unbelievably good value, Bali has mastered the art of hospitality. Developers go all-out to create well-equipped and enjoyable places to stay, with many hotels boasting splendid swimming and infinity pools that are destinations in their own right. Your perfect Balinese escape starts here.

Dubai

Dubai is an ideal holiday destination for families, with theme parks, beaches, Friday brunches and more to keep everyone happy. The famous Burj Khalifa, the tallest building in the world, is well worth the entrance fee. Burj Al-Arab, often touted as the world's only 7-star hotel, remains underwhelming. Similarly, the Aquaventure Water Park at the Palms Atlantis Hotel is not to be missed. Jumeriah Beach Residence is well established as Dubai's most popular beachfront, and its close proximity to a whole host of restaurants, cafes and shops make it more than worthy of its status.

Geneva

The charming city of Geneva comes with a plethora of attractions and tremendous natural beauty that makes every traveler fall in love with it. However, because of this overabundance of attractions, it becomes difficult for the backpackers to determine where to go first. Most of them generally miss out on all the best things that this fantastic city has to offer. The capital of Switzerland and a beautiful lakeside city, Geneva is home to many international organisations like the UN.

Port Blair

Port Blair is an alluring destination for tourists as it includes a host of attractive locales. There are shimmering but clean beaches, beaches that defy the ferocity of sea and let tourists swim leisurely, relics of colonial power and oppression, many must-go museums, libraries, coral reefs, and more.

Rome

The capital of one of the most powerful ancient empires in the world, Rome is a fascinating place that has inspired people to visit for millennia. With incredible works of art, a leisurely pace of life and world-renowned cuisine, the Eternal City is worth a visit at least once, though it would take a lifetime to see all it has to offer.
